Privacy Policy

Supremo Properties (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations.

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Personal identifiers – full name, email address, mobile number, landline (if provided)
  • Property-related information – preferred property type, location, budget range, preferred mode of payment, timeline for purchase

  • Technical data – IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time and date of access (collected automatically via cookies & analytics tools)

  • Directly from you when you fill out inquiry forms, send emails, message us on Facebook/Messenger, call, or meet with our agents

We use your personal information for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To process reservation requests and facilitate transactions with Vista Land
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • To comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, and enforce our rights

We may share your personal data with:

  • Vista Land & Lifescapes, Inc. and its authorized representatives (for processing reservations and documentation)
  • Banks and financing partners (only when you explicitly apply for in-house or bank financing)
  • Service providers (e.g., email service, cloud storage, analytics providers) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Government authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law (usually 10 years for real estate transaction-related documents).
